Will Roland

Will Roland (born March 5, 1989) is an American actor best known for originating the role of Jared Kleinman in the Broadway musical Dear Evan Hansen. He is also known for his role in the 2017 film One Penny. He was born in NYC and attended NYU where we graduated wih a singing degree.
